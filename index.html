ABOUT ME


I am Sugandh Bansal, I was born and raised in India. I have a teaching background in English and Art and I have worked in different parts of the world as a volunteer, subsitute teacher and private tutor (online and offline) for about 10 years. I have also worked in customer service for more than 4 years.

In the starting of Covid-19, when everyone sent home from work, I started my learning in Information Systems in 2019, and there I have discovered the world of Data Analysis. I realized how powerful data is to transform a business, making things easier and improving our everyday lives. Therefore, I have decided to specialize and start a new career as a Data Analyst.

While looking for opportunity to work as Data Analyst, I started a youtube channel for Python beginners and for those, who doesn't have any IT or coding background like me. I truly believe in lifelong learning! Every day is a good day to learn something new and it is never too late!

So, here I am.
